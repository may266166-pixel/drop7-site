玩法 / How to play

点一下试管把顶部同色的液体倒进另一根管子,把每种颜色归拢到一起——一管装满一种颜色即过关。卡关了点「提示」看下一步,或用「加管」多加一根空试管,「撤销」随时反悔。

Tap a tube to pour its top-colored liquid into another; gather each color together — fill a tube with one color to clear the level. Use Hint for the next move, Add Tube for an extra empty tube, and Undo anytime.

进度会保存吗? 会,关卡、金币自动保存在本机。

Can a level be unsolvable? No, every level is guaranteed solvable.
