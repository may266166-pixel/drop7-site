玩法 / How to play

用手指连接棋盘上同色的圆点,让线路铺满每一个格子即可通关。灯泡按钮可给出提示。

Drag to connect dots of the same color and fill every cell on the board to clear a level. Tap the lightbulb for a hint.

常见问题 / FAQ

进度会保存吗? 会,所有进度自动保存在本机。
Is my progress saved? Yes, everything is saved locally on your device.

需要联网吗? 不需要,全程离线可玩。
Do I need internet? No, the game is fully playable offline.

怎么恢复已购买的内容? 商店页点「恢复购买」。
How to restore purchases? Tap "Restore Purchases" in the Shop.
