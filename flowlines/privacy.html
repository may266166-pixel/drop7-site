中文

《一笔连线》不收集、不存储、不上传任何个人数据,也不进行任何追踪。

English

Flow Lines does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, and does not track you in any way.

Game progress, settings and purchase state are stored only locally on your device (iOS user defaults) and never leave it.

In-app purchases are handled by Apple's App Store; we never see your payment information.

This app contains no third-party ads and no third-party analytics SDKs.
