玩法 / How to play

点击蓄力,再次点击把糯米团子弹向天空。空中长按可冲刺,借助弹簧和云朵弹跳,飞得越远越好。沿途收集金币,回到「升级车间」把团子升得更强。

Tap to charge, tap again to fling the mochi into the sky. Hold to boost in mid-air, bounce off springs and clouds, and fly as far as you can. Collect coins along the way and spend them in the Garage to make your mochi stronger.

常见问题 / FAQ

进度会保存吗? 会,金币、升级与纪录自动保存在本机。
Is my progress saved? Yes, coins, upgrades and records are saved locally on your device.

需要联网吗? 不需要,全程离线可玩。
Do I need internet? No, the game is fully playable offline.

怎么恢复已购买的内容? 商店页点「恢复购买」。
How to restore purchases? Tap "Restore Purchases" in the Shop.
